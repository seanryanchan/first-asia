Our Promise

First Asia Manufacturing Corporation was organized in December 27, 1999 with Securities and Exchange Commission Registration Number A199918656. The company has complied with the requirements of Food and Drug Administration. First Asia Manufacturing Corporation secures licenses to operate as Cosmetic Laboratory and Household Hazardous Substances Manufacturer. Also, the company is accredited by Philippine Drug Enforcement Agency (PDEA) as manufacturer of household products (e.g. products containing with controlled chemicals). All products are manufactured in accordance with the Current Good Manufacturing Practice (CGMP) based on the directive given by FDA. Technical staffs are members of Philippine Society for Cosmetics Scientist (PSCS) and Chamber of Cosmetics Industry of the Philippines (CCIP), organizations that provide updates and technical information in cosmetic industry.
