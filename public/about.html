About First Asia

First Asia Manufacturing Corporation (F.A.M.C.) is a 100% Filipino-owned company based in Metro, Manila Philippines primarily engaged in Contract Manufacturing. Its products revolve around personal care and household products (e.g. hand-body lotion, liquid hand-soaps, shampoos, toilet bowl cleaner, etc.).

Founded in December 27,1999 by Mr. Eugene Lim (C.E.O), the company since then has made progressive growth whilst maintaining its brand promise of providing only the finest products and services for its customers.

The First Mission

We aim to provide the ease of creating personalized products; and together with our expert services, fully involving our customers with the complete manufacturing experience nationwide.

The First Vision

To be known as the manufacturing corporation who commits wholeheartedly in providing honest, quality, and the consistent products with ease.
